# 🏐 The VNL Draft

**Build your ultimate Volleyball Nations League roster. Can you go undefeated?**

🔗 **[Play now](https://ethansunjoonyang.github.io/The-VNL-Draft/)**

---

## How to Play

Each round, the game spins a random **nation** and **year**. You draft one player from that pool to fill a position on your 7-man roster. Fill all 7 slots and your team gets an overall rating — the higher, the better.

### Your Roster
Every team needs exactly:
- **1 Setter (S)**
- **2 Outside Hitters (OH)**
- **2 Middle Blockers (MB)**
- **1 Opposite (OPP)**
- **1 Libero (L)**

### Rerolls
You get **one nation reroll** and **one year reroll** per game — use them wisely.

### Modes
- **Classic** — 7 rounds, one pick per round
- **Draft** — more picks per round with position choices

## Player Database

The game features **260+ real VNL players** from 18+ nations, spanning **2018–2026**. Players are sourced from official FIVB Volleyball World rosters and verified by position and year.

Nations included: ARG, BRA, BUL, CAN, FRA, GER, IRI, ITA, JPN, NED, POL, RUS, SRB, SLO, TUR, UKR, USA, and more.
